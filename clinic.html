About the Clinic

CalmMind Clinic is a fictional mental health clinic created as part of the IT420 Computer Ethics course project. The clinic uses information technology to provide safe and ethical mental health services.

Objectives of the Website

  • Provide clear information about the clinic and its services.
  • Allow patients to request appointments online in an ethical way.
  • Protect patient data and privacy following computer ethics principles.
  • Ensure transparency through a clear ethical user agreement.

Main Functionalities

  • View general information about the clinic.
  • Read details about services and working hours.
  • Submit an online appointment request (demo only).
  • Read and accept the ethical user agreement before using the services.

System Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).
  • Internet connection for online access.
  • Device with a responsive screen (mobile, tablet, or laptop).

Services

The clinic offers individual counseling, family counseling, stress management, and academic pressure support. All sessions follow strict confidentiality and ethical standards.

Note: This website is for educational purposes only. It does not provide real medical advice or emergency services.
