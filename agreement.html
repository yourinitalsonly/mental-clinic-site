Ethical User Agreement

Before completing your booking request, you must read and agree to the terms below. These conditions ensure privacy, confidentiality, and ethical use of our system.

1. Privacy and Confidentiality

  • All personal information provided is confidential.
  • Our clinic uses secure systems to protect electronic records.
  • Sharing patient data without consent is prohibited.

2. Accurate and Honest Information

  • Users must provide correct and honest information.
  • Impersonation or false identity is not allowed.

3. Responsible Use of Technology

  • No attempts to hack or disrupt the website.
  • No harmful content or malware may be sent.
  • The system is to be used ethically and legally.

4. Respectful Communication

  • All messages to staff must be respectful.
  • Hate speech or harassment is not tolerated.

5. Data Protection and Security

  • Your data is never sold or shared unlawfully.
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Users must protect their own devices.

6. Limitations and Emergency Cases

  • This website is not an emergency service.
  • Call local emergency numbers in dangerous situations.

7. Agreement Confirmation

To proceed with your booking, please confirm that you have read and agree to the Ethical User Agreement.
