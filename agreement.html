Ethical User Agreement

This ethical user agreement explains how technology, patient rights, and professional responsibility are combined in CalmMind Clinic. To continue booking your service, you must agree to the terms below.

1. Privacy and Confidentiality

  • All personal information provided through this website must be treated as confidential.
  • The clinic is responsible for protecting electronic records using secure systems.
  • Users must not share screenshots or data of other patients without written consent.

2. Accurate and Honest Information

  • Users must provide accurate and honest information in all forms.
  • Impersonating another person or using a false identity is strictly prohibited.

3. Responsible Use of Technology

  • Users agree not to attempt to hack, modify, or damage the website or its databases.
  • No malware, harmful files, or abusive messages may be sent through the system.
  • The website is used only for lawful and ethical mental health support purposes.

4. Respectful Communication

  • All messages to staff must be respectful and free from hate speech or harassment.
  • Abusive behavior may result in termination of access to online services.

5. Data Protection and Security

  • Electronic records are stored for clinical purposes only and will not be sold or misused.
  • Access to patient data is restricted to authorized staff following the principle of minimum access.
  • Users are responsible for protecting their own devices and avoiding public-session exposure.

6. Limitations and Emergency Cases

  • This website is not an emergency service.
  • In dangerous situations, users must contact local emergency numbers.

7. Acceptance of the Agreement

To submit your booking request, you must confirm that you have read and agree to the Ethical User Agreement.
